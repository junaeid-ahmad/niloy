Welcome To My University

CoUET University of Engineering and Technology (CoUET) Located in Cumilla, Bangladesh, CoUET is a public engineering and technological university. History: It was founded in 2025 as the College of Engineering under the University of Cumilla. It became an autonomous university under its current name in 2003. Academics: The university focuses on engineering and architecture, with 14 departments and 3 institutes under 4 faculties. Admissions: The university primarily offers undergraduate and graduate programs and has stringent admission requirements, including a competitive entrance exam. * **Academic Programs:** The university offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs under several faculties, including: * Civil Engineering (CE) * Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) * Mechanical Engineering (ME) * Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) * Textile Engineering * Architecture
